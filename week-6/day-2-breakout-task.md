#### Goal

Practice creating new data with a form.

#### Steps

1. Complete what we built in lecture.
2. Build a form for adding a new record.
3. Store the form values in state.
4. Submit the form and add the new data to your app.
5. Make sure the new record appears on the page.

#### Extra practice if you want

- Clear the form after submit.
- Add a select input.
- Show a short success message.
